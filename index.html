Privacy Policy

Mishkat Al-Hoda Islamic App

Last updated: December 10, 2025

Package Name: com.example.meshkat_elhoda

1. Information Collection

Mishkat Al-Hoda ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

1.1 Account Information

When you create an account or use guest mode, we collect:

  • Email address (only for registered accounts)
  • Name or username for personalized experience
  • Account preferences and settings
  • Progress data (Quran reading progress, prayer tracking)
Guest Mode: You can use the app without registration. In guest mode, your data is stored locally on your device only and is not synced to our servers.

1.2 Location Data

The App requests location permissions to provide:

  • Accurate prayer times for your location
  • Qibla direction calculation
  • Nearby mosque locations

Location data is used only when the relevant features are enabled.

1.3 Usage Data

We collect anonymous usage data to:

  • Improve app performance and stability
  • Fix bugs and crashes
  • Understand feature usage patterns
  • Personalize your experience based on your interactions

2. Data Storage and Security

2.1 Registered Accounts

For registered users:

  • Email and account information are stored securely on our servers
  • Your progress and preferences are synced across devices
  • We use industry-standard encryption for data transmission

2.2 Guest Accounts

For guest users:

  • All data is stored locally on your device only
  • No data is sent to our servers
  • Data is not synced across devices
  • You can export your data before switching to registered account

2.3 Security Measures

  • SSL encryption for all network communications
  • Secure password hashing for registered accounts
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Compliance with data protection regulations

3. Required Permissions

3.1 Account Permissions

  • Account creation: Optional for enhanced features and cross-device sync
  • Guest mode: Available for local-only usage

3.2 Location Permissions

  • Fine Location: For precise prayer times and Qibla direction
  • Coarse Location: For approximate location when high precision is not needed
  • Background Location: For prayer time notifications when app is closed (optional)

3.3 Other Permissions

  • Notifications: For prayer time alerts and reminders
  • Vibrate: For silent prayer alerts
  • Internet Access: To sync account data and download content

4. Third-Party Services

4.1 Authentication Services

For registered accounts, we use secure authentication services to:

  • Verify user identity
  • Secure account access
  • Enable password recovery

4.2 Google Services

The App uses Google Play Services for:

  • App functionality and updates
  • Google Maps API for Qibla direction and mosque locations
  • Secure authentication (if using Google Sign-In)

5. Your Rights and Choices

5.1 Account Management

  • Create a registered account at any time
  • Use guest mode without registration
  • Switch between guest and registered accounts
  • Delete your account and all associated data

5.2 Data Control

  • Export your app data at any time
  • Disable any permission through your device settings
  • Clear app data from your device settings
  • Opt-out of notifications through app settings
  • Request deletion of your personal data

6. Children's Privacy

The App is suitable for all ages and focuses on Islamic education. For children under 13, we recommend using guest mode or parental supervision when creating registered accounts.

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary:

  • Registered accounts: Data is retained until account deletion
  • Guest mode: Data is retained on device until app uninstallation or data clearance
  • Inactive accounts: Accounts inactive for 2 years may be deleted

8. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices. We will notify registered users of any material changes via email or app notification. The "Last updated" date at the top indicates when this policy was last revised.

9. Compliance

This Privacy Policy is designed to comply with:

  • Google Play Store requirements
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
  • Industry best practices for mobile apps
